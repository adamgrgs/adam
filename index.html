Hi, I am a seasoned Marketer with more than 12 years building, managing, and monitoring marketing campaigns on almost every marketing channel.

At my last job at airbnb, I managed organic growth operations for all of the airbnb business. I started working at airbnb as a Performance Marketing Manager leading 2 teams of paid and organic search specialists for the lux business unit only. In less than a year I managed to ship some of the most ambitious and successful organic growth projects in the history of the company like the successful digital PR campaign of the airbnb LUXE launch, building a Knowledge Graph for all the high end homes, and expanding the brand’s digital footprint using data and UGC. About a year later I was promoted to oversee the organic growth operations for the whole airbnb business in the marketing HQ in Seattle.

As the Head of operations for Organic Growth Marketing my role was to ensure collaboration between cross-functional teams from data scientists, engineers, product, copywriters, researchers, and designers in project ideation, management and delivery. I was also responsible for representing the whole channel in front of the marketing and homes leadership teams in weekly scorecard meetings, MBRs, QBRs, and yearly reviews.  

Before airbnb, I worked for over 3 years at Paysafe (and Skrill digital wallet) to develop, manage, and evaluate digital marketing strategies, including SEO, PPC, SEM, content marketing, database email, and social media. I also hired, managed, and developed a team of driven, highly engaged individuals to manage marketing channels from different offices in North America and Europe.

My biggest successes at Paysafe were building a fully-fledged sales enablement CRM with lead-scoring and marketing automation capabilities. I also designed the digital marketing strategy for the Paysafe Platforms solutions with the first true go-to-market plan and well-researched tactics which helped us secure strategic channels like TechCrunch, Engadget, Forbes, and other major news and social media outlets. This launch campaign ended up being extremely successful, surpassing our conversion goals.

Outside of work, I am on the judging panel of the UK Search Awards and the Head Judge of the Canadian Search Awards.  

I am also a husband, a father, and an aikido instructor.
